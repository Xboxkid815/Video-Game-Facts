Video Game Facts

Roblox

Origins and Development

  • Rocket Arena: The very first game officially released on the platform when it launched in 2006 was a player-vs-player tower combat game called Rocket Arena.
  • Programming Language: All games and experiences on Roblox are created using Luau, which is a fast, small, and gradual typing system derived from the Lua programming language.
  • Roblox Studio: This built-in game engine generates millions of user-created experiences each year.

Players and Community

  • Creator Economy: Incredibly, developer payouts on the platform are massive. Many developers earn enough to pay for college tuition or even become millionaires.
  • User Stats: The platform is largely popular with youth demographics, with the company reporting that its monthly player base historically includes about half of all American children under the age of 16.
  • The "Dominus" Creator: The iconic and extremely rare Dominus in-game hat was originally created by Roblox developer and administrator Merely as a fun side project.

Gameplay Records

  • Brookhaven RP: This town and city roleplaying game is one of the most visited experiences on the entire platform, having accumulated tens of billions of visits.
