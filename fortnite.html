Fortnite

Fun Facts

  • The "Black Hole": In 2019, to transition between chapters, Epic Games deleted the entire game map, leaving players staring at a literal black hole for 36 hours before Chapter 2 launched.
  • The Speed of the Bus: By checking the default configuration files, fans discovered that the flying Battle Bus travels over the island at exactly 50 meters per second (111 mph).
  • Real-World Lawsuits: Epic Games was actually sued by the owners of a real-life location in Florida because the in-game POI "Coral Castle" shared the same name.
  • Carrying Teammates: You can carry knocked-out teammates while gliding if you pick them up after launching yourself out of one of the Steamy Stacks chimneys.
  • Music in the Game: The "Jug Band" emote, when fully harmonized by four players, plays the melody of Lady Gaga's "Poker Face".

Cultural Impact

  • A Virtual Concert Hall: Fortnite has hosted massive in-game concerts featuring artists like Travis Scott, Ariana Grande, and Marshmello, which drew tens of millions of concurrent virtual attendees.
  • The Avengers Clash: In 2018, Fortnite reportedly generated more revenue in a single year ($2.4 billion) than the global box office gross of the massive Marvel film Avengers: Infinity War ($2.04 billion).
  • Platform Pioneer: Epic Games successfully negotiated with Sony to introduce cross-platform play, fundamentally changing the industry by allowing PlayStation, Xbox, PC, and mobile players to all play together.
  • The Skin Boom: Fortnite features thousands of playable character skins, recently surpassing the total number of years since the fall of the Roman Empire.

Gameplay & Mechanics

  • Chests: You can locate hidden treasure chests by listening closely for an angelic choir sound.
  • Drowning: While it takes a long time, it is entirely possible to drown if you purposely trap yourself underwater until your health and shields completely deplete.
  • Weapon Rarity: Guns are color-coded by rarity to help you find the best loot. The scale goes from lowest to highest: Grey, Green, Blue, Purple, and Gold.
  • Rocket Riding: Riding an opponent's rocket across the map was originally a bug, but Epic Games decided to keep it in the game because players loved it so much.
